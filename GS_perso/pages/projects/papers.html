Kernel Organization Paper


Paper on Kernel Organization

During my studies at the IIT, I wrote a paper on the possible Kernel organizations for an operating system. Among them, I particularly described the monolithic and the microkernel wrote a quick overview of some exokernels and described the major operating systems now available. I compared these different solutions and discussed the trade-offs made in each of them.




IPv4 to IPv6 Network Paper


Paper on the Internet Protocol versions 4 and 6

In my Computer Networks class at the IIT, I make an overview of the famous Internet Protocol version 4 and 6. I describe their architectures and their features. I also comment IPv6 prospective in the upcomming years.




Big Data & Telecommunications Network Project Paper


Paper on a Software Project using Big Data and Cloud Computing

In this document, I describe the opportunities represented by Big Data solutions for Internet Service Providers and also present a fictive end-to-end process based on a software located in smartphones and tablets collecting data generated by the user, sending them to the Cloud where these data can then be processed by Hadoop, using MapReduce and Data Mining techniques, extracting relevant and meaningful information to provide personalized services to customers.




Big Data and Cloud Computing for Business Intelligence Perspective


Paper on Big Data, Cloud Computing and Business Intelligence

In my Advanced Computer Networks class at the IIT, I chose to write my final papers on the alliance of Big Data and Cloud Computing and their particular interaction with respect to Business Intelligence Problematics. In this paper, I take an in-depth study of these three major phenomenae, describe the opportunities that they represent and the interaction they can make in today's companies.
